About Cloud9

Cloud9 (C9) is an American professional esports organization based in Los Angeles, California. It was formed in 2013, when CEO Jack Etienne bought the former Quantic Gaming League of Legends roster.

During 2013, the Cloud9's League of Legends Roster was able to capture their first LCS title

Following the success of Cloud9's League of Legends team in the NA LCS in 2013, the team began expanding into other esports like CSGO, Overwatch, etc.
